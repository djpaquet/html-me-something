Milo The Crazy Pup

This is just a page dedicated to my crazy wonderful dog Milo

Early Days

When I first saw Milo at the Human Society I should of known right then he would be trouble, but he was so cute I just couldn't leave without him. There he was in his little kennel, jumping and barking like a litte maniac. 'I want that one!' I said to myself. He's been a handful ever since!


I adopted Milo in March 2017, he was 5 months 10lbs and scrappy!

Milo Does What Milo Wants

When Milo wants something he does not keep it a secret. Even after going through The Butt Sniffers Academy at The Watering Bowl, Milo still feels like the rules don't apply to him.



Some of his favorite things are:

  • Treats
  • Carrots
  • Pizza crusts
  • Causing a ruckus
Milo loves to play all the time! He espicially loves to play with his cousin Loki.

Can't Have Nice Things

Milo is the the Master of Distruction! He has chewed several shoes, broken a laptop, eaten several remotes. This is just the tip of the iceberg. Also I will say that there is no such thing as a indistructible toy, no matter what the package says!

He also seems to hate every dog bed I buy him. They will end up destuffed and shredded in no time.

He's Cute & He Knows It

I have to admit that I might be partially be to blame for Milo's behaivor. He's just so cute and I've spoiled him rotten!
Just look at his little face though! Can you blame me?


All in all I must say even I am so happy I brought this little rascal home and couldn't imagine life without him. If you're thinking about getting your own little scamp to love I suggest checking out your local shelter, they are filled of all types of wonderful animals waiting for your love.
